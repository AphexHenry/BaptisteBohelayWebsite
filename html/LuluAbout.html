Lulu's Unreal Exploration - About

Experimental video game

This game is like a laboratory where I experiment my video game ideas.


expect to be lost

You are following Lulu, in his exploration journey in a strange and eerie world. You will meet and play different creatures that Lulu meet during the adventure.


this creature will try lose your mind

This work has been done in an original way, every act is improvised and spontaneously created. It was very important for me not to explain how the user has to play and how he has to interpret the story. The goal is to keep a deconstructed story. I let the player creating the missing pieces, trying to make the experience different and maybe more personnal.



some puppet scenes help the player to make his own story


The visuals are minimalists, inspired from the shadow puppets and the expressionism artistic movement.


There is no rules in this game, no point, just the exploration



I am currently looking for some funding to continue the game.
