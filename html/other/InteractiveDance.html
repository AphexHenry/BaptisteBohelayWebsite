Interactive Dance Performance with Visuals


This is my first experience with both dance performance and a Kinect. The dancer is represented by particles and projected on a screen. He create flows that move the particles by moving his hands, thus create a disformed reflection of him self he can play with.



This performance has been presented at the Yukon Art Center, Whitehorse, in November 2013.
